THEMIS: Evaluating Multimodal Large Language Models for Scientific Paper Fraud Forensics

Code arXiv

Abstract

Recent advancements in generative AI have introduced significant challenges to academic integrity, particularly through the creation of sophisticated forged scientific images and texts. In this paper, we present THEMIS, a comprehensive benchmark designed to evaluate the forensic capabilities of Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) in detecting scientific paper fraud. Our benchmark covers various dimensions of manipulation...

The THEMIS Framework

The THEMIS framework evaluates models across three key dimensions: (1) Detection of manipulated regions in scientific figures, (2) Reasoning about the logical consistency between text and experimental data, and (3) Attribution of generative sources.
